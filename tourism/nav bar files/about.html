About TSTDC

Telangana State Tourism Development Corporation (TSTDC) was incorporated under the Companies Act on 28th August-2014.

Telangana State Tourism Development Corporation (TSTDC) is entrusted with developing tourist infrastructure, package tours for the overall development of tourism in the State.

It is involved in many areas of activities such as hotels, resorts, wayside amenities, tourist packages and boats etc. It has also ventured into new tourism-related fields like, Heritage, Pilgrimage, and Eco-tourism etc.

Major objectives of TSTDC

  • Tourism Infrastructure Development
  • Circuit / Destination Development
  • Destination Development
  • Promotion of New Tourism Products and Destinations

Current status of the Infrastructure:-

Hospitality: The Corporation has developed a chain of hotels and restaurants providing quality accommodation and food for tourists at important locations. The Corporation operates Haritha Hotels & Resorts consisting of Restaurant, Bar & Wayside amenities spread across the state.

Transport: The Corporation has one of the most modern transport fleet for any corporation in the country and operates a number of package tours to different destinations within and outside the state i.e, Daily, Weekly and tours on demand. The fleet strength includes Volvo, Mercedes Benz buses, AC and Non-AC Hitech buses, Minibusses (for Hop-on Hop-Off City sightseeing tours), innovas and one high-end customer-oriented CARAVAN vehicle. The fleet is operated through a wide network of information & reservation offices located in important cities and through the tour promotion scheme agents and authorized booking agents.

Water Fleet: The corporation operates leisure-based cruises and water sport boats at different lakes and rivers in Telangana. Boating activity is introduced at Peddacheruvu, Kinnerasani, Komati Cheruvu, Singotam, Lakkaram and Somasila-Srisailam. Corporation also operates boating activities from Nagarjunasagar Hill Colony(Pushkara Ghat) to NagarjunaKonda Museum.

Sound and Light Shows: TSTDC presents Sound & Light shows at historical fort –Golconda Fort at Hyderabad, Warangal fort and Elagandla fort, Karimnagar. The shows have recorded narrations (English, Hindi & Telugu) along with imaginative use of music, sound and light effects – creating a dramatic history of the forts.

Eco-tourism: The Corporation was the first to take up Eco-tourism initiative in the State. It identified lakes, caves, waterfalls, islands, hillocks, rivers and forests as the natural resources with tourism potential.

Cultural Festivals: TSTDC organizes state festivals – Bathukamma & Bonalu festivals. Extensive use of media and press for campaigning, creating awareness for its properties is also promoted by organizing festivals which would be held each year as a part of its calendar events.

Single Window Service: For the first time in India, TSTDC has introduced Customized Tours to fit the specific needs of tourists. The tour is customized on the requirement of choice of activities of the tourists and TSTDC which helps and plans the entire itinerary for providing a seamless tour experience.
